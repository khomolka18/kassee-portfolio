OBJECTIVE

Upgrading the DISH onboarding experience.

Signing up for DISH requires more than just a first bill, and customers can tend to get lost during the setup process. To decrease churn, I worked alongside our lead UX/UI designer and a lead strategist to create a seamless on-boarding experience for new DISH customers. Internally, we called it the 100 journey - because we're there every step of the way.

SOLUTION

Step by step, personalized support.

DISH launch a personalized, machine learning-enabled journey hub for new customers that recommend actions that maximize the customer's use of their TV package. Roojoom will recommend objectives based on completed customer actions, past recommendations that have been successful for similar customers, and KPI impacts for other customers who have completed the recommendation.

DEVELOPMENT

Working in parallel with our external dev team.

After the design was established, I worked closely with an outside development team to ensure that the app functioned smoothly and remained aligned with the original design goals. We hosted virtual walk throughs for our design system, beta reviews, testing analysis, and Q&A sessions over the course of a few months.

RESULTS

Success for our customers and the company.

Very simply, the customers that used the onboarding app were more engaged than customers who did not. We observed engagement increases in connectivity, MyDISH account usage, OnDemand usage, hardware upgrades, and a longer customer lifespan. We totaled at a net enterprise impact of over 2 million dollars in just 5 months after launch date.
