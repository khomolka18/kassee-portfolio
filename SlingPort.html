Sling Locals

Role: Lead UX & Design

Brand Director: Angela Fagg

UX/UI Director: John Lindmark

PROBLEM

An 85% success rate for a semi simple onboarding.

After many user interviews and analytics, we determined a few key themes. People were overwhelmed with the amount of information, confused by all the tech heavy language, and troubleshooting always sent them in circles. There were a lot of pain points along the way, especially when users had to exit the SLING app to complete certain tasks, such as finding a close broadcast tower or adjusting their phone's WiFi settings.

STRATEGY

Personalized and efficient flows, with an updated tone.

We streamlined the flows, and broke out the steps into more frames that were more easily digestible. This gave the users a clear direction and sense of accomplishment. We leaned into more consumer friendly language and only displayed pertinent information. We also used bottom drawers to give more support if needed, without crowding each frame. Lastly, we added more personalization to keep.

RESULTS

A 12% lift in first time success rate, and more.

We jumped from 85 to a 97% success rate on the first try. Success rate of all users who troubleshooted once was 99.5%. This means less time troubleshooting, running in circles, and resorting to calling support.
